Coinks↘ (2023)

A tiny city-building puzzle. With the jam theme "Roles Reversed", the catch was that you have to lose money as quickly as possible. The nice little optimal strategy that people usually only discover on the first attempt is to just buy expensive buildings instead of just using a money-burning one.

I was a designer and a programmer in a team of 7.
